Mapfile merger (for v8.1)

REQUIREMENTS TO USE

  • All URLs must be under the max limit.
  • Consecutive URLs must have the correct coordinates. e.g. if the first url ends at (0,0,-1000), the second URL should begin at (0,0, -1000) or slightly afterwards.
  • Endings in the 1st URL will be removed. If you have multiple endings in your level, please contact the developer on discord (or somebody else familiar with mapfiles) to add them back in.
  • URLs should begin with: https://icedodo.onionfist.com/singleplayer?mapUrl=
  • If you need to combine more than two URLs, contact the developer on discord.
